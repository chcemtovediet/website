## Fractions from Basics

Sample activity focused on understanding fractions.

### What are Fractions?

Fractions are a way to express parts of a whole. When we divide a pizza, an apple, or chocolate, we work with fractions.

### How We Teach Fractions

- Practical activities with real objects
- Dividing and comparing
- Visualization and manipulation
- Playful forms of learning

### Activity Examples

Children work with real objects that they can divide, assemble, and compare. Through play, they discover what half, third, quarter mean and how these parts relate to each other.
